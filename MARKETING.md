# Founder Growth Agent – Marketing & Launch Strategy

## Product Positioning

**Tagline:**  
> "The AI growth advisor that founders actually use."

**Long Positioning:**
> Founder Growth Agent is an AI-powered growth strategist specialized in founder-led B2B growth. Unlike generic AI tools, it understands the founder's dilemma: limited budget, limited time, unlimited ambition. Get personalized positioning, cold outreach sequences, content strategies, and CRM insights—all designed for solo founders and small teams.

**Key Differentiators:**
1. **Founder-Specialized** – Not generic AI; built for B2B founder growth
2. **CRM-Integrated** – Analyzes your real pipeline (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive)
3. **Zero-Budget Tactics** – Organic, founder-led growth strategies
4. **Real-Time Streaming** – Instant actionable frameworks
5. **Affordable** – $9/month vs. $5K+ for a growth consultant

## Launch Roadmap (First 90 Days)

### Phase 1: Soft Launch (Week 1–2)

**Objective:** Get first 100 free signups, validate messaging, collect founder stories

**Channels:**
- Product Hunt (founder-focused community)
- Indie Hackers (founder-led growth thread)
- Twitter/X (organic, founder-focused audience)
- Email to warm contacts (founder community, advisors)
- Slack: Indie Hackers, Bootstrap, SaaS communities

**Messaging:** "I built this because I had no money for marketing. Here's how I'm getting my first customers—AI-powered."

**Goal:** 100 free signups, 20 active users, 5+ founder interviews

---

### Phase 2: Community & Education (Week 3–4)

**Objective:** 500 free users, establish founder-focused positioning, build content

**Channels:**
- Twitter threads on "founder growth without budget"
- YouTube: 5-min walkthrough ("Build your growth strategy in 5 minutes")
- Blog: "I Got 10 Customers with $0 Budget—Here's How" (personal founder story)
- LinkedIn: Thought leadership on founder-led growth
- Discord/Slack: Indie Hackers, Bootstrap, No-Code communities

**Content Pillars:**
- "Zero-Budget B2B Growth: 7 Tactics That Work"
- "Positioning Your SaaS: A Founder's Guide"
- "Cold Outreach That Converts: Real Examples"
- "Why Founders Fail at Growth (And How to Fix It)"

**Goal:** 500 free signups, 10% trial conversion, 3+ pieces of content

---

### Phase 3: Paid Acquisition (Week 5–8)

**Objective:** 1,500 users, positive unit economics, founder testimonials

**Channels:**
- Google Ads (target: "founder growth," "B2B growth strategy," "startup growth")
- Twitter ads (audience: founders, indie hackers)
- Capterra/G2 profile (claim, get early reviews)
- Partner: Indie Hackers (sponsored launch post)
- Affiliate: Growth consultants, startup communities

**Budget:** $300/month test, scale to $1,500/mo if CAC < $30 and LTV > $150

**Landing Page Copy Focus:**
- Problem: "You can't hire a $5K/month growth consultant. But you need growth strategy."
- Social proof: "Used by 500+ founders getting their first customers"
- CTA: "Try Free – No Credit Card"

**Goal:** 1,500 signups, 80+ Starter subscriptions, LTV:CAC > 5:1

---

### Phase 4: Authority & Partnerships (Week 9–12)

**Objective:** 5,000 users, establish as founder growth authority, strategic partners

**Channels:**
- Webinar: "Founder-Led Growth: How to Get Your First 10 Customers" (free, 1 hour)
- Guest articles: Indie Hackers, Product Hunt, SaaS publications
- Podcast appearances: Founder/bootstrap podcasts
- Case studies: 2–3 founder success stories
- Community: Founder Discord/Slack (exclusive, active)

**Content Roadmap:**
- Week 9: "State of Founder Growth 2025" (original research)
- Week 10: Webinar + recording (YouTube)
- Week 11: First case study (founder who got 10 customers)
- Week 12: "100 Founder Growth Tactics" guide (lead magnet)

**Goal:** 5,000 signups, 200+ Starter subscriptions, featured in 5+ publications

## Marketing Messaging Framework

### For Solo SaaS Founders
**Problem:** "I have no marketing budget and no customers yet."  
**Solution:** "Founder Growth Agent gives you a personalized growth strategy in minutes. Get positioning, outreach sequences, and content ideas—then execute for free."  
**CTA:** "Try free – start your growth strategy today."

### For Bootstrapped B2B Services
**Problem:** "I'm doing everything myself, including sales and marketing. Where do I start?"  
**Solution:** "Let AI do the strategy. You focus on delivery. Get cold outreach sequences, positioning, and 7-day action plans."  
**CTA:** "Get your first growth strategy free."

### For Growth Consultants / Agencies
**Problem:** "I want to offer AI-powered strategy to my founder clients."  
**Solution:** "White-label Founder Growth Agent for your clients. White-label, API access, or embed."  
**CTA:** "Contact sales for partnerships."
