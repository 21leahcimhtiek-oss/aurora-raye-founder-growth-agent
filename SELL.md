# Founder Growth Agent – Sales Enablement Guide

## Executive Summary

**Founder Growth Agent** is an AI-powered B2B growth strategist designed for solo founders and early-stage teams. It generates organic growth tactics—positioning, messaging, content strategies, and outreach sequences—with zero marketing budget required. Real-time streaming responses deliver actionable frameworks and templates instantly.

**Target Markets:** Solo SaaS Founders, B2B Services, Bootstrapped Startups, Indie Hackers  
**Primary Use:** Growth strategy, content planning, cold outreach, positioning  
**Price Range:** Free–$29/month (immediate access, no credit card)  
**Free Tier:** Yes – Core features, community support

---

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Primary ICP: Solo Founders (SaaS, B2B Services)

**Characteristics:**
- Building SaaS or B2B service solo or with 1–2 co-founders
- Limited marketing budget ($0–$500/month)
- Need growth strategy, not just tools
- Active on LinkedIn or X/Twitter for distribution
- Looking for organic, founder-led growth tactics

**Buying Signal:** "I don't know how to get my first paying customers" or "I'm doing everything myself, including marketing"

**Close Rate:** High (60–80% with demo)  
**Sales Cycle:** 1 week (impulse purchase at $9/month)  
**Objection:** "Why not use ChatGPT for free?" → **Answer:** "ChatGPT is general; we're specialized in founder growth. You get frameworks, templates, and CRM integration that sync with your contacts—not generic advice."

## Pricing Strategy

### Freemium Model

**Free Tier** – Core features, community support
- All core AI prompts (positioning, content, outreach)
- Suggested prompts library
- Real-time streaming responses
- No CRM integration
- Use case: Solo founders evaluating, students, side projects
- Conversion path: First 10 growth wins → upgrade for CRM sync

**Starter** – $9/month
- All free features, plus:
- CRM integration (Merge API – sync HubSpot, Pipedrive, Salesforce contacts)
- Personalized outreach at scale (AI analyzes your pipeline)
- Priority email support
- 1 workspace / account
- **Conversion signal:** "I need to scale my outreach"

**Pro** – $29/month
- All Starter features, plus:
- Unlimited workspaces / team seats
- Advanced CRM analytics (patterns, recommendations)
- Custom branding (white-label option)
- Slack integration (daily growth tips, CRM alerts)
- Priority support (Slack)
- **Conversion signal:** "I'm building a team or reselling"

**Enterprise (Custom)** – Contact sales
- Dedicated support, custom integrations, API access
- White-label or embed in platform
- SLA guarantee
- **Conversion signal:** Contact sales@auroramarket.org

## Sales Talking Points

### Problem Statement (Discovery)
1. "How are you getting customers today?"
2. "What's your biggest blocker—budget, time, or not knowing what to do?"
3. "If you had a growth strategist for $9/month, what would change?"

### Solution Overview (Pitch - 2 min)
> "Founder Growth Agent is like having a growth advisor in your pocket. You describe your business, and it generates:
>
> - Positioning & messaging (so people actually get what you do)
> - Cold outreach sequences (DM/email scripts that convert)
> - Content strategy (LinkedIn/X playbooks)
> - 7-day action plans (step-by-step what to do first)
>
> And it learns from your CRM—it analyzes your contacts and tells you who to reach out to and what to say."

### Key Benefits
- **Saves time** – Strategy in minutes, not weeks
- **Affordable** – $9/month vs. $5K+ for consultant
- **Personalized** – Uses your business details, not generic templates
- **Actionable** – Real frameworks, not fluff
- **CRM-ready** – Integrates with HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive

### Closing Questions (Qualify for Free Trial)
1. "Are you actively trying to grow your customer base right now?" (If yes → move to free trial)
2. "Do you have a CRM with your contacts?" (If yes, highlight CRM integration for Starter upgrade)
3. "Would you prefer a one-time session with a consultant, or ongoing AI guidance?" (If ongoing → Starter pitch)

### Objection Handling

**"Why not just use ChatGPT?"**  
→ "ChatGPT is great for brainstorming. But Founder Growth Agent is specialized—it knows founder growth. It asks *you* questions to personalize strategy, integrates with your CRM, and gives you templates you can use immediately. Plus, it's cheaper per month than one ChatGPT Plus subscription."

**"I'm already using a growth consultant"**  
→ "Great! Founder Growth Agent complements that. Use us for rapid strategy drafting between consultant calls, content planning, and cold outreach—it's like having 24/7 junior strategist. Your consultant focuses on big decisions."

**"I don't have a marketing budget"**  
→ "Perfect—that's exactly who we serve. The free tier is unlimited. No credit card. If you want CRM integration later, it's just $9/month."

**"Does it work for [my industry]?"**  
→ "If you sell B2B (SaaS, services, consulting, etc.), yes. It specializes in founder-led organic growth—LinkedIn, X, cold outreach, positioning. If you're selling D2C physical products or B2C, it'll help but isn't as optimized."

**"Will you use my data?"**  
→ "No. Your data is yours. If you integrate your CRM, we only access it to give you personalized advice—we don't share or store it beyond your session."
