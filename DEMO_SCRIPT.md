# Founder Growth Agent – Demo Script

## Overview
This 10-minute demo script guides sales and customer success teams through a founder-focused demo. Customize based on founder's business type (SaaS, Services, B2B, etc.).

---

## Pre-Demo Setup (5 minutes before)

1. **Test environment**
   - App loads without errors
   - Demo prompts ready (positioning, cold outreach, content strategy)
   - CRM integration ready (if applicable)
   - API response streaming works

2. **Prepare talking points**
   - Review founder's stage (idea, pre-revenue, revenue-generating)
   - Have 2–3 use cases ready for their industry
   - Know Starter vs. Pro differences

3. **Set stage**
   - Screen share ready
   - Mute notifications
   - Have backup demo URL ready (https://founder-growth-agent.vercel.app)

## Tips for Success

1. **Listen to their pain** – What stage are they at? What's the biggest blocker?
2. **Use founder language** – "First 10 customers," "zero budget," "founder-led growth"
3. **Show personalization** – Run a prompt with their actual business (not generic)
4. **Don't oversell** – The value is in the strategy, not flashy UI
5. **Get them to take action** – "Sign up free and run this prompt" beats "let me know if interested"
6. **Emphasize speed** – Strategy in minutes, not weeks of consultant time
7. **Focus on founder stage** – This is for bootstrapped founders with limited budget, not enterprises
8. **Always get a next step** – "Sign up free," "run this prompt," "call in a week"—never end with "let me know"
